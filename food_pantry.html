Welcome to Food Pantry

Who we are

College students and staff are facing food insecurity at alarming rates. The Maize and Blue Cupboard offers an immediate, comprehensive solution for the U-M community. Through access to resources, educational opportunities, and compassionate support, we empower students to make informed, sustainable choices.

What we provide

The Maize and Blue Cupboard provides a wide range of essential resources to support the well-being of the U-M community. We offer a variety of food options, including fresh produce, dairy, meat, bread, and shelf-stable items like rice and canned goods. Additionally, we supply kitchen essentials such as dishes, cookware, and utensils to help students prepare meals.
